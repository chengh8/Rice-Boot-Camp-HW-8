Summary: Latitude vs Weather

Latitude vs. X was built to analyze whether the location of a city and weather conditions are correlated. Data was called from OpenWeather API and datasets from ~500 cities were collected.


After cleaning and sorting the city weather data, Matplotlib was used to create these visualizations.


For further analysis, please select from the Plot drop down in the top right or select a visualization
